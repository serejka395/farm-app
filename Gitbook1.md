# 3. Gameplay Mechanics

## ðŸŒ¾ Farming Loop
The core of the game is managing your **18 Plots** of land.
1.  **Unlock**: Plots must be purchased. The first 6 are free. The last plot costs **5 Billion ZEN** or requires Level 100.
2.  **Plant**: Choose from **25+ Crops** across 6 Rarity Tiers.
    -   *Strategy*: Plant **Wheat** (10s) for active play or **Void Pumpkin** (48h) for passive yield.
3.  **Water**: Water boosts growth speed by **1.6x**. Water charges are a limited resource, creating a sink for ZEN/SOL.
4.  **Harvest**: Collect crops to gain **XP** and **Inventory**.

**Q: Is this Free to Play?**
A: Yes! You can start, farm, and earn XP without spending a penny. Premium upgrades speed up progress but are not required.

**Q: How do I earn money?**
A: Currently, you earn "Potential Airdrop Allocation" via XP. In Phase 3, you will earn $F2E tokens which can be traded.
